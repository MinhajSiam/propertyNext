Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying an apartment with PropertyNext.

What is the booking amount for a new flat?

The booking amount varies by project but generally starts at 10% of the total flat value. The remaining amount can be paid in flexible, interest-free installments.

Are your buildings earthquake resistant?

Yes. All our buildings are designed by top structural engineers complying with the BNBC (Bangladesh National Building Code) to ensure maximum earthquake and wind resistance.
