Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying an apartment with PropertyNext.

What is the booking amount for a new flat?

The booking amount varies by project but generally starts at 10% of the total flat value. The remaining amount can be paid in flexible installments until the handover date.

Do you provide assistance with bank loans?

Yes, absolutely. PropertyNext is affiliated with major banks and financial institutions. Our legal and financial team will assist you in processing your home loan smoothly.

Are your buildings earthquake resistant?

Yes. All our buildings are designed by top structural engineers complying with the BNBC (Bangladesh National Building Code) to ensure maximum earthquake and wind resistance.

What happens if there is a delay in handover?

We pride ourselves on timely delivery. However, in the rare event of a delay beyond the grace period mentioned in the agreement, PropertyNext provides compensation to the buyer as per the contract terms.
