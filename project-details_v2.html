Project Overview

The Lumina Residencies is a hallmark of modern architectural brilliance designed for those who appreciate the finer things in life. Situated in the highly sought-after location of South Kafrul, Dhaka, this luxury development ensures seamless city connectivity while providing a highly secure and serene living environment.

Every apartment is meticulously crafted according to the BNBC (Bangladesh National Building Code) standards, ensuring maximum natural light, cross-ventilation, and optimal space utilization. Experience a lifestyle upgraded by international standard fittings, modern eco-friendly features, and our signature 0% interest payment plans.

Technical Specifications

We compromise on nothing. The Lumina is built using the finest materials sourced globally and locally.

Structural Features

  • Earthquake resistant RCC frame structure.
  • BSRM/KSRM 500W steel reinforcement.
  • Holcim/Shah specialized cement.
  • Anti-termite treatment in foundation.

Flooring & Finishing

  • 24"x24" Premium Mirror Polish Tiles (RAK/Akij).
  • Solid Teak wood decorative main entrance door.
  • Berger/Asian Paints smooth plastic emulsion.

Kitchen & Bathrooms

  • European standard sanitary wares (Grohe/Kohler).
  • Granite countertop with stainless steel sink.
  • Hot & Cold water provision in all bathrooms.

Electrical & Elevators

  • 2 High-speed elevators (ThyssenKrupp/Hyundai).
  • Full capacity standby European Generator.
  • Fire-resistant concealed copper wiring (BRB).
